About us

We are a group of developers in formative stage, with the objective of developing a web information system aimed at gathering the information of the products of the Colombia Purchase and Sale Houses to publish their products, as well as how to use the viability of the information, so that people can access in an easier and more comfortable way the services offered by commercial houses.




Purchases

On our page you can access the products published by the commercial companies affiliated with CV System, if there is an item of your interest, you can make the reservation of the product online.

Pawn

Through the CV System you have the option of making an item that you no longer use, through our service, the request of commitment will be notified to all Commercial Houses that are affiliated, with this we guarantee that you receive more than one offer and have the option to choose the one that best suits your need.

Sales

In the same way that you request a pawn service, you can make the sale of a product that you do not use under the same methodology; Also, you can receive several offers of products published by the commercial houses, opting for the one that interests you the most.
